Chart Style Preview: Before vs After

Side-by-side comparison of the proposed color/visibility changes for the Analyzer chart.

BEFORE (Current)
Issues: Green candles are hollow outlines, hard to distinguish from background. Entry/exit arrows are tiny (4px) at 70% opacity. Session shading is 3% opacity — invisible. Volume bars at ~19% opacity. Trade PnL zone uses very light pastel fills.
AFTER (Proposed)
Changes: Green candles are solid filled. Entry arrows are larger (8px) at full opacity with a white border. Exit dots are larger (7px) with a glow ring. Session shading at 12% opacity with visible border. Volume bars at 50% opacity. PnL zone fill at 25% opacity with thicker connecting lines (2.5px).
