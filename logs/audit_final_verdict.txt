HIGH ISSUES (should fix, but paper trading can proceed with caution):
  1. Client Portal "volume" is HTTP snapshot count, not real trade volume.
     Poisons sweep volume confirmation, regime detection, VWAP, and delta.
     Sweep scoring degrades but FAILS SAFE (lower scores → fewer entries).
     Confirmed by Cross-Check 1 (upgrade from MEDIUM to HIGH).
  2. bid_volume, ask_volume, delta permanently zero in live IBKR path.
     Delta divergence signals in SignalAggregator are silently disabled.
     Two IndividualSignal contributions (delta_divergence_bearish/bullish)
     are dead code in production. Found by Cross-Check 1 (new finding).
  3. Dashboard kill switch is cosmetic — does NOT call emergency_flatten()
     or halt the real trading loop. Operator pressing it gets false
     confirmation while trading continues. Documented by Agent 3,
     escalated by Cross-Check 4 to "false sense of control."
     WORKAROUND: Use process SIGINT/SIGTERM as the real emergency stop.
  4. No IBKR_LIVE_CONFIRMED flag exists. Mitigated by live execution
     raising NotImplementedError. Must be addressed before live deployment.
  5. TWS ibkr_client.py place_order() has zero safety checks. Latent risk
     if TWS migration proceeds without adding safety rail integration.

MEDIUM ISSUES (fix during paper trading phase):
  1. Warmup phase relies on real-time bars (28+ min for ATR-14 priming).
     No historical backfill request. Safe — HTF gate blocks during warmup.
  2. ATR=0 produces stop_distance=0 which passes NaN guard and max-stop
     gate. No explicit zero-stop rejection. Found by Cross-Check 3.
     RECOMMENDED FIX: Add "if raw_stop <= 0: reject" guard.
  3. Contract resolution returns None on failure (soft failure, not exception).
     Caller checks for None. Acceptable but could be stricter.
  4. Gateway disconnect: 5-minute heartbeat halt window. Positions protected
     by 30-second reconciliation loop. Conservative but adequate.
  5. Dashboard kill switch daily reset clears is_halted at session open.
     Intentional design but could re-enable after catastrophic ETH loss.
     Safety_rails persistent trip requires manual reset (defense-in-depth).
  6. ALLOW_UNVALIDATED env var not checked against paper_mode.
     Could theoretically allow unvalidated instrument in live mode.
     Mitigated by live execution NotImplementedError.

LOW ISSUES (cosmetic or future improvement):
  1. full_backtest.py missing HTF gate for sweep entries (backtest only).
  2. MIN_RR_RATIO not in config/constants.py (defined locally in main.py).
  3. TradingView CSV import accepts zero-volume bars.
  4. session_type enum-to-string conversion is fragile.
  5. Paper trading journal export is manual (not in shutdown sequence).
  6. order_log.json has no log rotation (negligible growth rate).
  7. Reject-style comparisons (< threshold) slightly less readable than
