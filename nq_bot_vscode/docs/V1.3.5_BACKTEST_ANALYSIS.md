## BUG IN RESULTS: COMMISSION ANOMALY

**71 trades classified as C3-blocked but charged full 5-contract commission.** These are C1 12-bar fallback and maintenance flatten scenarios where C3 entered but was retroactively blocked. The system overpaid $549.54 in commission. This needs investigation in the executor's C3 entry/block logic to determine if:

1. C3 actually entered and its PnL was removed but commission wasn't refunded, OR
2. C3 never entered but was charged commission anyway

Either way, the accounting is wrong and should be fixed.

## CONCRETE RECOMMENDATIONS (PRIORITY ORDER)

### 1. Raise Minimum Stop Distance to 12+ Points
Trades with stops under 10 points are net losers (-$1,003 total). They're noise-level stops getting whipped out. Set a floor of 12 points minimum. This would eliminate ~500 losing trades and improve PnL by roughly $1,000+ while reducing commission drag.

### 2. Add Ranging Regime Gate
Ranging regime averages $0.46/trade across 646 trades. Either:
- Raise HC score threshold from 0.75 to 0.80 in ranging regime
- Require stronger HTF bias confirmation when ranging
- Skip ranging entries entirely in ETH sessions

### 3. Add Late-Afternoon Time Gate
Entries between 3-5 PM cost $2,047. Consider no new entries after 2:30 PM ET during RTH, or raise the score threshold to 0.85+ for late-session trades.

### 4. Fix C3-Blocked Commission Anomaly
71 trades are overcharged. Need to either refund C3 commission when C3 is retroactively blocked, or ensure C3 commission isn't charged until C3 entry is confirmed.

### 5. Reduce C3 Dependency (LONG-TERM)
The system cannot survive without C3. Ways to reduce dependency:
- Improve C2 structural targets (currently 29.1% of C2 exits hit target, 59.8% hit stop)
- Consider a wider C2 stop or a delayed C2 breakeven trigger
- Explore a C2 trailing mechanism (mini-trail before C3's wider trail)
- Investigate whether C1's 5-bar time exit is too aggressive (maybe 7-8 bars with a tighter profit threshold)

### 6. Fix the Two Critical Bugs from the Audit
- **C2 time stop divergence** (orchestrator uses 20, executor uses 35) — will cause live/backtest mismatch
- **C3 commission sign error** in `full_backtest.py` — inflates "savings" calculation

## FINAL VERDICT

**V1.3.5 is profitable but fragile.** The edge is real (walk-forward stable, all gates protecting), but it's thin (PF 1.18, $9.98/trade expectancy) and entirely dependent on C3 runners. The system trades like a trend-follower disguised as a scalper — it bleeds on C1 and C2 while waiting for C3 to catch a runner.

**Is it ready for live?** Not yet. The two remaining critical bugs (C2 time stop divergence, commission sign error) need to be fixed first. After that, paper trade for at least 2 weeks to verify live execution matches backtest behavior, especially C3 trailing fill quality.

**The good news:** Walk-forward shows the edge is getting stronger over time, not weaker. The gating system is well-calibrated. The C3 delayed entry concept saves $110K and is validated. These are solid foundations to build on.
